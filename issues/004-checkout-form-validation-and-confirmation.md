## What to build

Build the checkout form and confirmation flow from the PRD. Customers should be able to submit a preorder request with contact and fulfillment details, receive clear validation errors, and see a confirmation state with order number and final total after a successful submission.

## Acceptance criteria

- [ ] Checkout requires customer name and a valid phone number.
- [ ] Customers can choose WhatsApp, Telegram, Instagram, or phone as their preferred contact method.
- [ ] Customers can choose pickup or delivery.
- [ ] Delivery address is required only when delivery is selected.
- [ ] Customers can provide a preferred date that respects the preparation lead-time rule.
- [ ] Customers can provide optional notes.
- [ ] Validation errors are shown near the relevant fields.
- [ ] A successful order shows a confirmation state with order number, submitted items, and final total.
- [ ] The cart clears after a successful order.

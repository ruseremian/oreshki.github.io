## What to build

Create the cart path described in the PRD: customers can add catalog products and variants, adjust quantities, remove items, view line totals and subtotal, see pickup versus delivery pricing, track progress toward free delivery, and understand that payment is arranged manually after order submission.

## Acceptance criteria

- [ ] Customers can add products and variants to the cart from product cards.
- [ ] The cart can be opened from navigation and shows all selected items.
- [ ] Customers can increase, decrease, and remove cart items.
- [ ] Cart line totals and subtotal update as quantities change.
- [ ] Pickup shows no delivery fee.
- [ ] Delivery applies the configured fee below the free-delivery threshold.
- [ ] Delivery becomes free at or above the free-delivery threshold.
- [ ] The cart clearly states that online payment is not active.

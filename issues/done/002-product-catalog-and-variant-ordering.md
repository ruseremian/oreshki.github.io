`issues/prd.md`

## What to build

Build the product catalog experience from the PRD so customers can browse sweet bakery items and savory specialties, inspect product photos, descriptions, quantities, prices, availability, and select orderable variants such as pelmeni fillings, vareniki fillings, and cake flavors.

## Acceptance criteria

- [ ] Products are grouped into clear sweet and savory categories.
- [ ] Each visible product includes a photo, title, description, quantity or unit, and price.
- [ ] Variant products are selectable as independently orderable options.
- [ ] Unavailable products cannot be ordered.
- [ ] Product IDs referenced by content resolve to valid catalog entries.
- [ ] Product catalog data remains structured and reusable by cart, checkout, and order APIs.

## What to build

Add approved bilingual allergen and home-kitchen guidance before customers submit preorder requests. The guidance should be visible enough to inform customers with allergies without overwhelming the product browsing or checkout experience.

## Acceptance criteria

- [ ] French storefront content includes approved allergen/home-kitchen guidance.
- [ ] Russian storefront content includes equivalent approved allergen/home-kitchen guidance.
- [ ] Guidance appears near products and/or checkout before submission.
- [ ] Customers with allergies are directed to contact the bakery before ordering.
- [ ] The guidance does not replace product descriptions or change product prices/business rules.
- [ ] Tests or content checks verify the guidance exists in both languages where practical.

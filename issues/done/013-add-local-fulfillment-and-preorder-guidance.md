## What to build

Add approved local fulfillment and preorder guidance across the customer journey. Customers should understand that delivery is available in Strasbourg and nearby communes by confirmation, pickup is generally in Strasbourg with exact details confirmed after request, checkout uses a three-day minimum preorder rule, urgent requests go through direct contact, and submission is a preorder request rather than a confirmed order.

## Acceptance criteria

- [ ] Product/contact sections mention local delivery in Strasbourg and nearby communes by confirmation.
- [ ] Product/contact sections mention pickup in Strasbourg without exposing an exact public address.
- [ ] Checkout shows fulfillment guidance near the pickup/delivery choice.
- [ ] Checkout and confirmation copy consistently frame submission as a preorder request received.
- [ ] Three-day preorder guidance is consistent with the preferred-date validation rule.
- [ ] Urgent closer-date requests are routed to direct contact in copy, not checkout.
- [ ] Tests or content checks cover the key fulfillment/preorder messages where practical.

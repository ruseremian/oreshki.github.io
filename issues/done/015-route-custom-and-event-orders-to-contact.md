## What to build

Make the customer journey clear that custom quantities, family/event orders, and bespoke requests should go through direct contact rather than the standard catalog checkout. The normal checkout should remain focused on catalog items and quantities.

- [ ] Checkout remains catalog-only and does not introduce custom-order fields.
- [ ] The guidance is available in both storefront languages.
- [ ] Tests or content checks verify the custom/event guidance exists where practical.
